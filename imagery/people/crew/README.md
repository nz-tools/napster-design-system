# Crew & Lifestyle

**Slot:** Warm Lifestyle — User Value

## What's in this folder

Customer-in-scene photography. Real environments — living rooms, kitchens, home offices, studios, gyms. Used when the story needs a human anchor rather than a product render.

## Treatment

Treatment: warm amber-magenta grade. No stock photography. No models — humans in real environments doing real things. Beam overlay only on hero treatments.

## Contents

- Productivity crew in living rooms and offices
- Music crew making something together
- Consumer lifestyle (guitar, mobile, at the desk)
- Enterprise crew workshops
